# NextNest — Problem Statement

NextNest is a **privacy-first, multi-agent AI platform** that builds a transparent support ecosystem for orphanage children in India, bridging care gaps **before and after age 18** while fully complying with **DPDP Act 2023** and **JJ Act 2015**.

## What We Offer

- **Real-time, auditable funding dashboards** for donors and CSR partners  
- **Individual child sponsorship** with consent-based tracking  
- **Verified critical illness crowdfunding** with encrypted medical progress updates  
- **AI-driven tools with human oversight and bias audits** that:
  - Detect eligibility for government schemes (e.g. PM CARES, state scholarships)
  - Automate secure applications
  - Use **OCR-assisted document management** on anonymized data only  
- **Predictive analytics** (explainable models) that monitor academic and behavioral risks via **aggregated, non-identifiable indicators** for early interventions  
- **Aging-out youth support**: opt-in career guidance, job matching via partner networks, affordable housing linkages, and mentor connections — all with **granular privacy controls**  
- **Achievement portal** that fosters donor engagement **without exposing personal data**

## Impact We Deliver

- Reduce funding leaks by **up to 50%**  
- Unlock **80% more** welfare benefits  
- Prevent post-18 homelessness and vulnerability  
- Empower **data-driven** orphanage decisions  

## The Crisis We Address

**1.5 lakh youth** exit institutional care in India every year into vulnerability — with no safety net, no job, no housing, no mentor. At the same time, billions of rupees in welfare funds go unclaimed due to documentation gaps and scheme unawareness.

NextNest brings together donors, CSR partners, orphanage staff, government bodies, and AI to plug these gaps — with **explainable AI**, **human oversight**, and **privacy as non-negotiables** from day one.
